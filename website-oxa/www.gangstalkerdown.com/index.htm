Welcome to the archive of press articles about massacres where the suspect claimed to be a victim of gang stalking.

Dr. Kenneth Westhues is Professor Emeritus of Sociology, University of Waterloo, Canada. In nearly every mass shooting, Westhues found that a programmatic system of workplace or academic bullying had systematically isolated, scapegoated and humiliated the victim and virtually groomed the shooter for the role of monster. The role of psychiatry, police, and administrative bureaucracy as threat assessment teams and safety prevention was not only an utter failure, but, moreover, prompted the very acts of mass violence they sought to prevent.

Mass murder is often in the news. There are more and more shootings. The average number of victims becomes greater.

School shootings are frequent in the USA. See also www.schoolshooting.org.
