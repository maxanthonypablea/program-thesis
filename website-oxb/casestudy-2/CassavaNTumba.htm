The Kenyan journalist Cassava N’Tumba visits Dr. John Austin–Walker with a story about implants.
He has X–ray photographs to back it up.
The X–ray examination was performed at Brook Hospital Main, September 16, 1992.
Dr. Austin–Walker is so impressed that he contacts Prime Minister John Major.

A copy of the letters and the X–ray are all over the internet, usually presented as fact.

Henry Azadehdel.

  • Armen Victorian.
  • Cassava N’Tumba.

Henry Azadehdel was from Armenian descent.
He was a shop assistant in Nottingham in Great Britain.

John Lundberg made The Mythologist, a 26 minute documentary about Henry Azadehdel.
It was first broadcast on BBC FOUR in 2004.

John Lundberg made a website: www.mythologist.co.uk with more hoaxes from Henry Azadehdel.
